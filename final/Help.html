Accessibility:
Waterpark is a place where everyone can have fun. And people with extra assitance are allways welcome here. We have special arrangment for the people using wheelchair. If you need any assistence or more information about assessbility call us on: 1-800-89898-99990
Lost or Found:
If you found anything you can handover to the nearset stall or food stall. or if you lost anything you can go and report it at the welcome center near the entrence.
Maps:
You can find maps at every ride counter and if you don't find any map at the ride counter then you can find it near the restroom.
